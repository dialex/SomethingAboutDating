STEP 1 — What awaits you
Description:
  Dating is a journey with four stages:
  - You'll **meet** people and some will catch your interest.
  - You'll go on **dates** to learn more about them and see how well you get along.
  - You'll find a **match** and start an exclusive relationship.
  - You'll **share** a happy life for as long as it works for both of you.
Tips:
  - Prepare. Don't improvise or rely on luck.
  - Your behaviour moves *the spark* up or down. Once it drops below a certain point, it cannot come back.
  - If someone rejects you over a trivial trait, you dodged a bullet. Personality beats superficial traits.

STEP 2 — Recharge your body and mind
Description:
  Dating will be exhausting so ensure are refreshed before you get out there.
Tips:
  - Sleep 8 hours on a consistent schedule.
  - Get some sunlight every day, even briefly.
  - Move your body daily. Anything counts.
  - Stop using porn, alcohol and other drugs as emotional substitutes.
  - If you're struggling, get support (therapy, medication) before jumping into dating.


STEP 3 — Sort your appearance
Description:
  Present the **best version** of yourself. The basics go a long way.
  - Dress in simple, good quality clothes that fit and feel comfortable.
  - Smell nice and look clean.
  - Shave or trim head, face and body hair to your taste.
  - Cut and clean your nails.
Tips:
  - Avoid worn out clothes, hoodies, ripped jeans, and sports sneakers unless they're a deliberate style choice.
  - Men. A plain polo with jeans works. Match belt and shoes.
  - Women. A dress or skirt with clean lines works.


STEP 4 — Build your confidence
Description:
  Everyone is insecure. **Confident people just don't show it.** Fake it till you make it. Expect the first hundred attempts to feel awkward, then it will get easier.
Tips:
  - Maintain eye contact. Looking away shows insecurity.
  - Slow down physically. Relax posture, shoulders back, don't hesitate.
  - Stay calm, don't let anxiety show. No nervous laughs. Never use self-deprecating jokes.
  - Don't be an emotionless brick. Be vulnerable later, once trust is there.
  - Ignore charisma gurus. They make simple things sound complex to take your money.


STEP 5 — Polish your social skills
Description:
  Be someone your date is proud to be seen with: polite, respectful, treat others as equals, behave yourself.
  Respect their preferences. Also pay attention to their body language, not just their words: joy, discomfort, hot, cold, looking away, etc.
  Humor always wins. You don't need to do a stand up comedy act or memorise jokes. Just keep the mood light and playful.
Tips:
  - Say "please" and "thank you".
  - Be a little vague, make them want to ask.
  - Calibrate your jokes. Stay away from online memes.
  - Give unexpected or exaggerated answers to casual questions.


END STATE
If you look presentable, smell nice, are mentally stable, treat people with respect,
and carry a bit of confidence and humour, you're already ahead of half the population.
Ready for the next phase.
